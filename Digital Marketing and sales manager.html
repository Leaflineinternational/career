Digital Marketing and Sales Manager

Salary: ₹20,000 – ₹40,000 per month + Annual variable component for the right candidate

Company Overview

Leafline International, established in 2023, is a prominent manufacturer and exporter of bags based in Delhi NCR. We cater to both domestic and international markets with a strong focus on quality, sustainability, and design innovation.

Job Description

We are looking for a dynamic and versatile Digital Marketing and Sales Manager who can strengthen our online presence while also supporting offline sales and operational activities. This is a hybrid role combining ecommerce, marketing, sales, and operations.

Key Responsibilities

  • Digital Marketing & Content Creation
    • Enhance and maintain the company website and product catalogue
    • Create images, videos, and promotional creatives
    • Optimize content for SEO and engagement
  • E-commerce Listing & Management
    • Manage listings on Amazon, Flipkart, Meesho, and other platforms
    • Maintain SKUs, pricing, descriptions, and specifications
    • Monitor orders, inventory, and customer queries
  • Campaigns & Performance Tracking
    • Plan and execute digital campaigns (Google, Facebook, Instagram)
    • Track metrics and prepare daily / weekly performance reports
    • Be accountable for campaign ROI and budgets
  • Store Operations & Administration
    • Handle walk-in customers and offline sales
    • Support order fulfilment and inventory management
    • Assist with office coordination, compliance, and documentation
  • Product Research & Sales Growth
    • Research new product ideas and market trends
    • Analyze data to identify winning products

Skill Requirements

  • 0.5 to 3 years of experience in digital marketing, ecommerce, or sales
  • Proficient in computer usage; programming knowledge is a plus
  • Strong English communication skills
  • Graduate or Post Graduate (Technology background preferred)
